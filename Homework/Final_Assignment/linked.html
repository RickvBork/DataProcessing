Storytelling

Using the linegraph a user can select a given day in 2016, and the temperatures registered by a certain KNMI station on the selected day.

The barchart crossrefernces the temperatures of the selected day and station, with data from 1963. This year was chosen as it was one of the coldest years on average in recent Dutch history.

It is clear that during the winter, 1963 was especially harsh, with minimum temperatures below -15 degrees Celsius. However, during the rest of the year, 1963 is almost comparable to 2016 regarding temperatures.

In november 2016 is often considerably colder. A surprising find given the record setting 1962-1963 winter months, showing how divers a single year can be regarding temperatures.
